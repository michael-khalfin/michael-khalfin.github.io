About

I'm a fourth-year undergraduate at Rice University graduating in December 2026 with a B.S. in Operations Research and a B.A. in Mathematics. I spent Spring 2024 studying abroad at Budapest Semesters in Mathematics, where I deepened my understanding of discrete mathematics and combinatorics.

Currently, I'm conducting research on online algorithms with Professor Sebastian Perez-Salazar and working as an Operations Research intern at Sandia National Laboratories. My research focuses on decision-making under uncertainty, competitive analysis, and data-driven optimization methods.
